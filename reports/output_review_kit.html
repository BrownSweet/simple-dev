Review Flow

  1. Open /Users/brown/program/www/person-skill/simple-dev/reports/output_blind_review_pack.md or this kit and compare Variant A vs Variant B for each case.
  2. Record choices in /Users/brown/program/www/person-skill/simple-dev/reports/output_review_decisions.json without opening the answer key.
  3. Use winner_variant A or B, confidence from 0 to 1, and a short reason for every case.
  4. Set reviewer_attestation truthfully after choices are recorded and before adjudication.
  5. Run python3 scripts/yao.py output-review-import --input <reviewer-decisions.json> --blind-review-attested --run-adjudication after choices are recorded.
  6. Refresh python3 scripts/yao.py review-studio . before asking for release approval.

Privacy

  • The answer key is intentionally withheld from this kit.
  • Do not inspect hidden labels or expected winners before decisions are recorded.
  • Do not paste private user data into decision reasons.
  • Pending decisions must stay pending instead of being counted as human agreement.
